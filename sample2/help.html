Help & Support

Asked Question

How do i borrow equipment?

1. Login to your account
2. Go to Borrow page
3. Select equipment and quantity
4. Submit the form

How do i return equipment?

1. Go to Return page
2. Go Enter your student details
3. Select equipment to return
4. Submit the form

What if i lose equipment?

Contact the Sports Department immedietely for inquires.

How long can i keep equipment?

Equipment can be borrowed for up to how many days you put.
